Hurricane Warning System

An android app to detect the reach of various hurricanes affecting the world. This uses real time data collected by satellites and weather organizations from across the word. Incident Organizing committee.

Incident is the annual cultural festival of the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) in Surathkal, Karnataka, India. The fest is a five-day-long event during the even semester of the NITK Calendar held in the month of March. The fest brings together students from different colleges all over India and is composed of several competitive events as well as pro-shows by big artists including Sunidhi Chauhan, Salim-Sulaiman, the duo of Vishal-Shekhar and Javed Ali. Incident is belived to be the second biggest cultural fest in India (after Mood Indigo) due to the sheer scale and number of events hosted.
